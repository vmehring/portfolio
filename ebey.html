Ebey's Knoll

This was a project of the architecture firm, Zai, Inc. It was to be a housing development that supported aging-in-place. Universal design principles were appled throughout the project, in the homes and in the landscape.

As the firm's landscape designer, I was responsible for researching and implementing universal design practices to the landscape plan. I created graphics for investor relations, resolved site grading, and established finished floor elevations.
