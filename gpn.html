Gateway Park North

This was my Masters thesis. The site is a park on the east shore of the Duwamish River in the Georgetown neighborhood of Seattle. It's also at the street-end of 8th Avenue South.

The Duwamish River is infamous for being highly polluted. It is a Superfund site. In spite of this, wildlife survives here, including salmon. This design for Gateway Park North includes salmon habitat in the intertidal zone, and gathering spaces for humans in the upland portion of the site.
